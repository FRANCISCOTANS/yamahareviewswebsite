Yamaha Model Descriptions

  • Aerox: A sporty scooter designed for speed and style, perfect for young riders who want a dynamic look and agile performance in city traffic.
  • NMAX: A premium urban scooter known for its comfort, fuel efficiency, and advanced features, making it ideal for daily commuting.
  • R15: A compact sport bike inspired by Yamaha’s racing DNA, offering aggressive styling and high performance for thrill-seekers.
  • MT15: A naked street bike with a bold design and responsive engine, great for riders who want a mix of power and urban maneuverability.
  • Sniper: A sporty underbone motorcycle built for agility and speed, popular among riders who enjoy both city and spirited rides.
  • Fazzio: A stylish and modern scooter with a retro touch, designed for convenience, fuel economy, and easy handling in urban settings.
  • Mio Gear: A rugged and practical scooter made for tough roads and daily use, offering reliability and versatility for all riders.
  • YZF-R3: A lightweight supersport motorcycle with a powerful engine, perfect for those who want to experience track-inspired performance on the road.
  • YTX 125: A simple, durable, and affordable motorcycle, ideal for business, delivery, and everyday transportation needs.
  • MT-03: A dynamic naked bike with a strong engine and agile handling, suitable for riders stepping up to bigger bikes.
  • WR155R: A dual-sport motorcycle built for both on-road and off-road adventures, offering durability and versatility for explorers.
  • XSR900: A retro-modern motorcycle that combines classic styling with cutting-edge technology and high performance for enthusiasts.
